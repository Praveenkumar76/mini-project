About Us

Welcome to Clinical Guidance System, Where healthcare and technology unite for a brighter, healthier tomorrow..

Our Vision

We believe that healthcare information should be available to everyone, not just as a luxury, but as a basic right. Our journey started with one simple idea: to give people the knowledge and tools they need to better manage their health.

Who We Are

We are data science students working on a mini project with a shared goal: to make healthcare information more accessible and easier to understand. Even though we are a small team, we have a strong passion for using data and technology to improve healthcare.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a simple, user-friendly platform that uses artificial intelligence to help you understand your health. By entering your symptoms, location, climate, our system predicts potential health issues and provides educational resources to increase your health knowledge.
